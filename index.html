What is AI's Impact on Psychiatry?

Diagnosis Improvement
-Artificial intelligence such as PredictX helps identify antidepressant medications through deep-data analysis 

Treatment 
-Honey Naps created SOMNUM to detect sleep patterns with artificial intelligence and help create solutions for insomnia related disorders

Workload Reduction
-Artificial intelligence is easily automating certain parts of being a psychiatrist, lessening the workload for real human psychiatrists.

Future of Psychiatry with AI
-Psychiatrists may have to watch out as artificial intelligence could become too advanced and remove the need for human psychiatrists

What is PredictX?

Predictx is a new artificial intelligence service that speeds up the diagnosis process for people suffering with depression. You might think this is not needed because there aren't a lot of antidepressant types. However, while there is only five major types of antidepressants, those are just categories, each category has several different antidepressants. PredictX's diagnosis allows patients to get the proper medication they need in record time. Not only is it beneficial for patients, but psychiatrists as well as it lessens their workload and they can focus on other aspects of their job.

What is the average wage?

One of the many benefits of becoming a psychiatrist is the hourly wage that can go up to around $110 and an annual wage which can range from $226,000 to $282,000 a year not including any potential bonuses. Of course you need to work extremely hard to reach this point as your starting salary may not reach these numbers, and your pay heavily depends on where you live. (state, country, firm, etc.)
